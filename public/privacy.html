Privacy Policy

We built Today because we believe your thoughts belong to you, and only you.

01. No Data Collection

Today is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data.

  • No analytics tracking
  • No user accounts
  • No cloud synchronization
  • No telemetry

02. Local Storage

All your journal entries, settings, and metadata are stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data. If you uninstall the application without backing up your data, it will be permanently lost, as we have no copy of it.
